About Me

My mission as a Certified Addictions Counsellor is to provide a grounded, compassionate space for individuals seeking to navigate the complex path of recovery. I believe that recovery is not just about abstinence, but about rebuilding a life of purpose, connection, and dignity.

Every client brings a unique story and set of challenges. My approach is rooted in client-centered care, ensuring that your voice and your goals remain at the heart of our work together. By utilizing evidence-based strategies and professional clinical expertise, we move beyond generic wellness toward meaningful, sustainable change.

How I Can Help

Support built around structure, accountability, and real change.

"This work is not about motivation. It's about structure, accountability, and real change. The level you choose should reflect how serious you are about changing your life."

What the Service Is

  • A structured, session-based coaching service
  • Delivered through 1:1 sessions (primarily virtual, with option for in-person)
  • Clients typically attend 2 or more sessions per week
  • Services are offered in set program durations (4, 8, or 12 weeks)
  • Emphasis on accountability, routine-building, and real-life execution

What the Service Is Not

This service is not clinical therapy and does not include:

  • Mental health diagnosis
  • Psychotherapy or clinical counseling
  • Medical or psychiatric treatment
  • Crisis or emergency services

Terms of Service

Services Provided

Steve Miller Recovery Services Ltd. provides addictions counselling and recovery coaching by a Certified Addictions Counsellor (CCAC). Services are delivered virtually or in-person within British Columbia, Canada.

Not a Substitute for Emergency Care

The services offered are not a substitute for medical treatment, psychiatric crisis intervention, or clinical therapy. If you are experiencing a medical or psychiatric emergency, contact 911 or your local crisis line immediately.

Client Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate information relevant to your counselling goals.
  • Attend scheduled sessions or provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations.
  • Engage in good faith with the recovery process.

Fees & Payment

Session fees are listed on this website and are payable in Canadian dollars. Fees may be updated periodically with advance notice. Package pricing is available and non-refundable once sessions have commenced.

Cancellation Policy

Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice may be charged in full. Repeated no-shows may result in discharge from the program.

Limitation of Liability

Steve Miller Recovery Services Ltd. is not liable for outcomes resulting from the application of advice or strategies discussed during sessions. Counselling is a collaborative process and results vary by individual.

Disclosures

Professional Designation

Steve Miller holds the designation of Certified Addictions Counsellor (CCAC) through the Canadian Addiction Counsellors Certification Federation (CACCF). This designation requires ongoing professional development and adherence to a code of ethics.

Scope of Practice

Services are limited to addictions counselling and recovery coaching. Steve Miller is not a licensed physician, psychiatrist, psychologist, or registered clinical counsellor. Services provided do not include:

  • Medical diagnosis or treatment
  • Prescription of medication
  • Psychiatric assessment
  • Clinical psychotherapy

Regulatory Framework

This practice operates in compliance with applicable British Columbia legislation, including the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) and the Business Practices and Consumer Protection Act.

Crisis Resources

If you are in crisis or need immediate support:

  • BC Crisis Line: 310-6789 (no area code needed, 24/7)
  • Canada Suicide Prevention: 9-8-8 (call or text anytime)
  • Emergency Services: 911

Conflict of Interest

Steve Miller does not receive commissions, referral fees, or incentives from any third party for recommending products, services, or treatment programs. All recommendations are made solely in the client's best interest.
